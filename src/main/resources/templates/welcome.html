So, what exactly does CerePro do?

CerePro hooks into Help Center Suggestion Box feedback submissions and analyzes the text to answer questions like: "How do Pros feel about the cost of PFC/PPC/CC? Three processes are used for this.

Sentiment Analysis

Determines the sentiment of a feedback submission and computes its magnitude. Magnitude is how much emotion is present in the text.

Entity Extraction

Determines what people are actually talking about in their submission and assigns relevancy based on the structure of the text.

Entity Sentiment Analysis

Calculates the sentiment relative to a mentioned entity. This helps us understand differing sentiment between mentions within a single submission.
